# Missing/Checkerboard Texture Replacements - 900+ Lore-Friendly Textures (Male & Female)

![thumbnail](https://cdn.static.foo/fd_missing_textures/fd%20clothing.png)

While similar replacements exist, they are often not lore-friendly or miss prop textures entirely. This pack features full male and female fixes for missing/checkerboard textures, including over 900+ custom-made variations.

## ðŸ’¾ Installation

Drag and drop the `fd_missing_textures` folder into your resources. The included **ydd and images** folders are optional extras; move them to your desktop or a safe place, as they do not need to be streamed.

Loading order in your `server.cfg`: 
```
ensure fd_missing_textures
ensure [clothing]
```

**Note:** if you don't have a subscription [https://portal.cfx.re/subscriptions/element-club](https://portal.cfx.re/subscriptions/element-club). then use `sv_maxclients 8` in your settings.cfg
